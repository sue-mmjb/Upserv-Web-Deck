5. DETERMINING THE WINNERS

The Contest shall have four (4) winners (collectively, “Winners” and each individually a “Winner”), one for each prize category, selected as follows:

  • BOLDEST IDEA ($15,000): Awarded to the submission that demonstrates the highest degree of creativity, originality, and inventive thinking. Judging criteria include: (i) Uniqueness of concept (does this idea feel fresh and original?); (ii) Creative problem-solving (does it approach game day in an unexpected way?); (iii) Inventiveness (does it make us think “why didn’t anyone think of this before?”). Sponsor reserves sole discretion to determine the winner based on these criteria.
     

  • BEST BUILT ($15,000): Awarded to the submission that demonstrates the highest quality of design, functionality, and user experience. Judging criteria include: (i) User experience design (is the app intuitive and easy to use?); (ii) Interface quality (is the design polished and visually coherent?); (iii) Functionality (does the app work as intended without errors or confusion?); (iv) Attention to detail (does it feel complete and thoughtfully built?). Sponsor reserves sole discretion to determine the winner based on these criteria. Technical complexity is not a requirement; quality of execution is prioritized over scope.
     

  • MOST BUZZWORTHY ($15,000): Awarded to the submission that demonstrates the highest level of user engagement and organic reach during the Entry Period. Judging criteria include: (i) Total unique users who accessed or interacted with the app (“unique users” means distinct individuals); (ii) Social media engagement (shares, mentions, and organic reach); (iii) Community interest and discussion generated. The winner will be determined by a combination of verified user analytics and social traction metrics. Sponsor reserves the right to audit user counts and disqualify entries with fraudulent or artificially inflated metrics.
     

  • WILD CARD ($5,000): Awarded at the sole discretion of the judging panel to a submission that stands out, regardless of category. Factors that may influence selection include, but are not limited to: (i) Memorable or distinctive quality; (ii) Unexpected approach or execution; (iii) Any other attribute that captures the judges’ attention. Sponsor reserves sole discretion to determine the winner. The judges’ decision is final and no explanation or justification is required.

Judging Process: Winners will be selected by a panel of judges appointed by Sponsor, which may include Sponsor’s employees, advisors, and external experts. All judging decisions are final and binding. Sponsor is not obligated to provide feedback or scoring details to non-winning entrants.
 

Subject to verification of eligibility and compliance with these Rules, each potential winner will be declared the official Winner of their respective category. A single Entry may win only one (1) prize category. For the avoidance of doubt, Sponsor cannot guarantee the exact time during the Judgment Period in which Entries will be reviewed.
